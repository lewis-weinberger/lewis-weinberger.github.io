Research

The Epoch of Reionization

I am an astrophysics PhD student interested in modelling the evolution of the intergalactic medium (IGM) during cosmic reionization; in particular how we can use the Lyman alpha and 21 cm spectral lines of hydrogen to probe the ionization state of the IGM. I am also interested in high redshift galaxies. To explore how these systems evolve I make use of numerical simulations, such as the Sherwood suite.

Reionization animation

Volume rendering of the reionization phase transition. This is the process in which the neutral hydrogen in the IGM (leftover from recombination) is ionized by early UV sources (most likely galaxies).

I made this animation using outputs from a post-processed simulation run by Girish Kulkarni. He used the ATON radiative transfer code to post-process the Sherwood IGM simulations. The video is a bit jumpy because the simulation snapshots were saved every ~40 Myrs.
